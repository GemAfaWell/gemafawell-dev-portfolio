Who am I?

Hey! I'm Gemini, an early-30s software engineer finding the love of code a decade and a half way too late. I spent a lot of time in a lot of careers (account management, education, customer success and sales, in that order), and even half a decade in the gig economy, learning first-hand about how to change the world without the logic, and coding has added the missing piece for me. I love building, and I love to bring joy to folks - here, I can put both together. Excited to grow wherever I need to grow and build something that changes the world!
